    Within every sentient being lies a soul, and one can give that soul to the darkness or to the light. In order to understand the secrets of the demons one must give their soul to the darkness.
    
    Only a true demon can permanently contain a dark soul. If one is not a demon they must first shed their mortal flesh if they desire to harness the fullness of the power of dark magic. When one has given their soul to the darkness they can enter into, and take control of, the body of another. When within that body they can leach their life force to replenish their own.
